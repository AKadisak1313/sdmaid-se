I-SD Maid 2/SE isesha idivayisi yakho futhi iqhathanisa amafayela nezinhlelo zokusebenza ezifakiwe ukuze ikunikeze izinketho zokukhulula indawo yesitoreji ngokuphepha.

Uma izinhlelo zokusebenza zakha amafayela ngaphandle kwamafolda azo aqokiwe, amafayela angasala ngisho nangemva kokukhipha izinhlelo zokusebenza. Ithuluzi le-"CorpseFinder" lithola izinsalela zohlelo lokusebenza, likutshele ukuthi zaziyiziphi izinhlelo zokusebenza, futhi likusize ukuzisusa.

üßπ Susa amafayela angadingeki nama-cache afihliwe
Izithonjana, imigqomo kadoti, ama-cache angaxhunyiwe ku-inthanethi nokunye: Uma izinhlelo zokusebenza zingazihlanzi zona, lolu hlelo lokusebenza luzokwenza. Ithuluzi le-"AppCleaner" lithola izinhlelo zokusebenza ezinamafayela angadingeki.

üì¶ Phatha zonke izinhlelo zakho zokusebenza
Thola uhlu oluphelele lwazo zonke izinhlelo zokusebenza ezifakwe kudivayisi yakho. Uhlelo lokusebenza olunikiwe amandla, olukhutshaziwe, lomsebenzisi noma lwesistimu: alukho uhlelo lokusebenza olungakufihlela. Ithuluzi le-"AppControl" lingumphathi wohlelo lokusebenza okuvumela ukuthi useshe, uhlele futhi ulawule izinhlelo zakho zokusebenza.

I-"StorageAnalyzer" ingumphathi wefayela obonisa ukuthi isikhala sisetshenziswa kanjani kudivayisi yakho, okwenza ukuphathwa kwesitoreji sakho kube lula.
